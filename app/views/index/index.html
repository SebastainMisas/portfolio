The 8 stage development process

1) Discovery, I meet with you and lay out a detailed feature set.
2) Models and architecture, the development team sets up early infrastructure for applications.
3) Wireframes, the skeleton of your application, conceptualized.
4) High fidelity mockups, high Definition renderings of your website or application.
5) Scaffolding/Models the first stage development work.
6) Front end and backend development work
7) Revisions, tesing and optomizing
8) The handoff, deployment! Launch! See your website or application go live!
